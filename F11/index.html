All right one day I was sitting at my Windows 2000 workstation and I hit the F11 key expecting all of my open windows to slide away to reveal the desktop. Obviously, this didn't happen but for a good half second or so I couldn't figure out why.

Don't let it happen to you!

F11.exe puts an icon in the System Tray, activating the desktop reveal whenever you press the F11 key. A tap of the F11 key will toggle the desktop reveal, but if you hold the key down, the windows will come back to the center when you release.
